# Artemis Astro Project

## Project Vision
- Corporate, simple, and "cool" defense industry website.
- Focus: Drones, Cybersecurity, Satellites, Anti-Drone Systems.
- Colors: Primary #182132, Secondary #eeeae1.

## Mandates
- **Mobile First:** All development must prioritize mobile responsiveness and touch-friendly interactions before scaling up to desktop.
- **Aesthetic:** High-tech, VC/Corporate feel with subtle animated elements (radar, grids).
